Tabs

The tabbable UI Component includes many positioning and styling options, starting with minimalistic tabs to vertical or horizontal tabs, left, center or right aligned and more. Tabs are responsive and the component automatically displays a horizontal scrolling area when using many tabs on a single tabbable widget.
